[h1]✦ Clean Slate[/h1]

A Quality of Life mod for Stellaris that adds tools for resetting, reorganising, and managing your empire's planets and infrastructure.

[quote]All features are player-only and designed to complement any playstyle without touching AI behaviour.[/quote]

[hr][/hr]

[h2]Features[/h2]

[h3]Decisions[/h3]

[b]🧹 Clear World[/b]
Removes all buildings and districts from a planet, resetting it to a blank colony ready for redevelopment. The appropriate capital building is automatically restored based on your empire type.

[list]
[*][b]Cost:[/b] 500 Unity
[*][b]Enactment Time:[/b] 30 days
[*]Works for regular empires, hive minds (including wilderness), machine empires, and habitats
[/list]

[b]👥 Relocate Population[/b]
Resettles all pop groups on a planet to other planets within the same sector. If the planet is the only one in its sector, pops are sent to a random planet elsewhere in your empire. A faster way of abandoning a colony.

[list]
[*][b]Cost:[/b] 25 Influence, 500 Unity
[*][b]Enactment Time:[/b] 30 days
[*]Requires at least one other planet in your empire to resettle to
[/list]

[b]🏗️ Rapid Construction (Toggle)[/b]
A per-planet toggle that dramatically increases building speed at the cost of proportionally higher building costs. Automatically upgrades when you research the relevant engineering technology.

[b]🏚️ Demolish Habitat[/b]
Permanently destroys an empty habitat and refunds a portion of the alloys used to build it. The habitat must have no population before demolition can proceed.

[list]
[*][b]Cost:[/b] 500 Unity
[*][b]Enactment Time:[/b] 30 days
[*][b]Alloy Refund:[/b] 500 alloys (mechanical shipsets) / 250 alloys (biological shipsets)
[*]Removes all associated megastructure components from the system
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Edicts[/h3]

[b]🧬 Accelerated Genetic Modification[/b]
Infuses genetic modification facilities with exotic gases to accelerate genetic changes across your population.

[list]
[*][b]Activation Cost/Upkeep:[/b] Exotic Gases (scaled by empire size)
[*][b]Effect:[/b] +50 flat sub-species integration, +300% sub-species integration speed
[*]Requires: Gene Tailoring technology
[/list]

[b]☠️ Accelerated Purge Protocols[/b]
Keeps undesirable populations in a compliant state using exotic gases, dramatically accelerating purge operations empire-wide.

[list]
[*][b]Activation Cost/Upkeep:[/b] Exotic Gases (scaled by empire size)
[*][b]Effect:[/b] +100% pop purge speed
[*]Requires: At least one species being purged
[/list]

[hr][/hr]

[h3]Defines[/h3]

[b]💸 Reduced Colony Abandon Cost[/b]
Reduces the influence surcharge for abandoning a colony (resettling the last pop) from [b]200[/b] down to [b]25[/b].

[hr][/hr]

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]

This mod is fully additive — it does not overwrite any vanilla files. All content is new decisions, edicts, and a single defines override for the colony abandon cost.

Any mod that also overrides [b]RESETTLE_DESTROY_COLONY_COST[/b] in defines will conflict on that value.
